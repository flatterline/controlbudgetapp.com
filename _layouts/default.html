Control is the simplest budgeting tool since cash.

Control Budget Application displays how much spending money you have on hand without the need to carry cash.

Using a cash budget is the simplest and most effective way to limit your spending. Cash gives you an immediate sense of how much money is available each time you open your wallet however we all know having cash on hand can be dangerous and messy. Cash is easily lost or stolen. By using Control you have access to the same benefits of carrying cash without the risk of having your untraceable cash stolen. Control also keeps track of your available spending in a single whole number without breaking your money down into smaller pieces, saving you time and money.

Intuitive design means you already know how to use Control before you even open this iOS application the first time.
